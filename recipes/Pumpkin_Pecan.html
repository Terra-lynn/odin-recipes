Pumpkin Pecan Cupcakes with Cream Cheese Frosting

Description

Who doesn't love cupcakes? And who doesn't love cream cheese frosting? Well, if you are like me, you are going to love These pumpkin pecan cupcakes with cream cheese frosting. Each cupake is loaded with pecans and delicous pumpkin flavor, topped with a creamy cream cheese frosting with a hint of pumpkin pie spice. Can you say yum!

Ingredients

Pumpkin Pecan Cupcakes

  • 3 cups all purpose flour, unsifted
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1 15 oz can Pumpkin
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/4 stick of butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Beat eggs
  4. Add sugar to eggs
  5. Add oil and mix ingredients
  6. add dry ingredients to wet ingredients
  7. stir in pecans
  8. Spoon into cupcake tin
