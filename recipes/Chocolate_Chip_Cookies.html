Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

These easy Chocolate chip cookies are the chocolate lovers' answer to cookies. A favorite among almost everyone, these cookies are easy to make, especially with step by step instructions and simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Large Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Oatmeal ground to fine powder
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Table Salt
  • Semi-sweet Chocolate bar, grated
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Chopped Walnuts

Steps

  1. Combine wet ingredients
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Make the dough
  4. Add the mix-ins
  5. Bake
  6. Cool
