Red Velvet Cupcakes

Home

Description

One of the prettiest treats you can make for a celebrationor gathering is red velvet cupcakes. It's easier for people to just take one! A good recipe to have because its unique!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Unsweetned Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter, room temperature
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Sour Cream
  • Vanilla
  • Milk
  • Red food coloring
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Beat butter and sugar
  4. Add eggs, sour cream, milk, and mix together
  5. Add red food coloring
  6. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix
  7. Add to cupcake liner and bake
