Pumpkin Cheesecake

Home | Chocolate Chip Cookies | Blueberry Muffins

Description

This double-layer pumpkin cheesecake recipe makes a creamy cheesecake base topped with a layer of spiced pumpkin cheesecake filling on a graham cracker crust. A great alternative to traditional cheesecake — especially for pumpkin fans. With two layers of creamy goodness, this pumpkin cheesecake is an indulgent dessert that’s sure to be the star of your Thanksgiving dessert table. Rich, velvety, and perfectly festive, it’s a pumpkin cheesecake guests will ask for year after year.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • Graham cracker crust
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground cloves
  • Ground nutmeg
